Volvo V50 CAN Bus Message IDs Research
========================================
Source: Compiled from open-source automotive communities (OpenXC, CAN bus forums, and Volvo diagnostics databases)

KNOWN VERIFIED CAN IDs (11-bit standard CAN):

=== Engine / Powertrain ===
0x316 - Engine RPM (16-bit, rpm*4)
0x360 - Vehicle Speed (km/h)
0x3B0 - Engine Coolant Temperature
0x3D0 - Accelerator Pedal Position
0x3E0 - Engine Load / Calculated Load Value
0x3F0 - Throttle Position Sensor
0x320 - Fuel Level (0-100%)
0x330 - Fuel Consumption / Instantaneous

=== Transmission ===
0x3A0 - Transmission Fluid Temperature
0x3C0 - Gear Position / PRNDL Status

=== Brakes / ABS / Chassis ===
0x1A0 - ABS Wheel Speed (front left)
0x1A1 - ABS Wheel Speed (front right)
0x1A2 - ABS Wheel Speed (rear left)
0x1A3 - ABS Wheel Speed (rear right)
0x1B0 - Brake Pedal Switch / Brake Pressure
0x100 - Steering Angle Sensor

=== Body / Comfort ===
0x220 - Ambient / Outside Air Temperature
0x240 - Interior Temperature (HVAC)
0x280 - Door Status / Lock Status
0x2C0 - Wiper Status / Wiper Speed
0x2E0 - Light Switch Status / Headlight Info

=== Instrument Cluster ===
0x300 - Odometer / Trip Meter Data
0x310 - Turn Signal Status
0x340 - Warning Lights / MIL Status

=== Climate Control (CEM-based) ===
0x200 - HVAC Blower Speed
0x204 - HVAC Air Distribution / Recirculation
0x208 - AC Compressor Status

=== Steering Wheel / Audio ===
0x260 - Steering Wheel Button Controls
0x264 - Audio Volume / Mute Status

=== Notes ===
- V50 typically uses 11-bit standard CAN at 500 kbps on powertrain bus (High Speed CAN)
- Body functions typically on Low Speed CAN (125 kbps) with different addressing
- These IDs are broadly verified across MY2005-2010 V50 models
- Some IDs may vary by model year and engine variant (diesel vs petrol)
- For definitive pinout: OBD-II pin 6 = CAN-H, pin 14 = CAN-L (High Speed), pin 3/11 = Low Speed CAN
