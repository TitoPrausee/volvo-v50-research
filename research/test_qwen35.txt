## P1 Platform Overview
Vehicles: Volvo C30 (2007-2013), S40/V50 (2004-2012), C70 (2006-2013)

## CEM Connector Overview
The P1 CEM sits behind the glovebox. It has multiple connectors (typically labeled A, B, C, D, E, or numbered depending on revision).

### Main Connectors (CEM-Low and CEM-High variants)

**CEM A-connector (large, 54-pin) — Power, Ground, LIN bus, CAN bus:**
- Pin A1: Battery power (30) — always on
- Pin A2: Ignition power (15) — switched
- Pin A3-A6: Ground (31)
- Pin A7-A8: CAN-H / CAN-L (drivetrain CAN)
- Pin A9-A10: CAN-H / CAN-L (body CAN/comfort CAN)
- Pin A11-A12: LIN bus 1, LIN bus 2

**CEM B-connector (medium, 26-pin) — Lighting & signaling:**
- Pin B1: Left turn signal output
- Pin B2: Right turn signal output
- Pin B3: Brake light output
- Pin B4: Reverse light output
- Pin B5: Rear fog light
- Pin B6: Position/parking lights (left)
- Pin B7: Position/parking lights (right)
- Pin B8: License plate light

**CEM C-connector (large, 40-pin) — Wiper, washer, horn, HVAC:**
- Pin C1: Horn relay control
- Pin C2: Windshield wiper (speed 1)
- Pin C3: Windshield wiper (speed 2)
- Pin C4: Rear wiper (if equipped)
- Pin C5: Washer pump
- Pin C6: Blower motor control

**CEM D-connector (20-pin) — Door modules, interior lighting:**
- Pin D1-D4: LIN to driver/passenger door modules
- Pin D5-D6: Interior lighting (dome/courtesy)
- Pin D7-D8: Sunroof control (if equipped)

**CEM E-connector (16-pin) — Immobilizer, keyless, auxiliary:**
- Pin E1-E2: Immobilizer antenna ring
- Pin E3-E4: Keyless entry receiver
- Pin E5: Siren control
- Pin E6: Hood switch

## Important Notes
1. Pinouts vary between model years (pre-2008 vs post-2008 facelift).
2. CEM part numbers changed across production runs — always verify against your specific CEM.
3. The P1 CEM has "CEM-Low" and "CEM-High" variants depending on options.
4. Official pinout data requires Volvo VIDA/DICE subscription or a wiring diagram manual.
5. Community resources: swedespeed.com forums, matthewsvolvosite.com, volvoforums.com have user-verified pinouts.
